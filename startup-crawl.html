STRIVE
STARTUP
CRAWL

The Strive Startup Crawl is a 1 day event where you have a front-row seat to meeting the hottest startups and building a network that’ll launch your career.

1. Visiting Startups

The first part of the crawl consists of groups of participants travelling across town to different startups' offices, and talking with the startup founders.

2. After Event

The second part is a networking session that happens after the visitings where all participants and startups come together to one place and mingles.

3. Matchmaking Session

The last part is connected with the networking session where participants can book one-on-one meetings with the startups' or hiring teams for career prospects.
