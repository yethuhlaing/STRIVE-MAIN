OUR ENDGAME

Daily, we see potential students making incredible work in their own fields, while startups transform our world. We believe that there is a symbiosis between these two that will accelerate positive impact as a civilization. It’s hard to just watch from the side-lines knowing this synergy can exist, so we’re here to facilitate that. We see a future of young builders thriving in meaningful work, building the next big thing at startups, and growing as people. We’ll do everything in our power to achieve that. That is STRIVE’s endgame.
